Car Race

The Car Race Game is about you driving a car backwards on a road, and trying not to get hit. This app requires mobile players to play in a new tab.

How To Use for PC: W and S, Up and Down to change speed, A and D and Left and Right to move car. How to use for Mobile: Click the orange button to start, use the joystick to move.

Alien Battle

The Alien Battle game is a game I made for a captsone project. it is about 2 UFO's fighting eachother until one wins.

How To Use 1 player: use W, A, S, D to move, space to fire at one side, alt to fire at another side. How to use 2 player: use the arrows to move, enter to fire at one side, delete to fire at another side.
