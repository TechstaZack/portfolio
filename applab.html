Calculator For Shapes And Math

The Calculator App is a app that can Calculate the area and perimeter of a rectangle/square, and a normal calculator for multiplication, addition, subtraction and division. This project is usable on all devices, except toasters.

How To Use: click shapes/maths, then type in the information in the boxes, then click the respective button.

Weight Calculator For Other Planets

The Weight Calculator works by multiplying you current Weight to the gravity of the planet. This project works on all devices.

How To Use: type in your current weight in the "Wheight on Earth" box. Weight must be in KG.
